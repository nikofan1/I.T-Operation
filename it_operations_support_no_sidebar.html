IT Operations & Support – Overview

Operational & Support in IT

The backbone of an organization’s technology

What Is This Field? Role in Organizations Responsibilities Key Tools

What Is This IT Field?

This field covers the management, maintenance, and support of tech devices and systems that organizations use every day. The main goal is to ensure businesses run smoothly without disruption.

Explain Its Role in Organizations

IT Operations & Support serves as the backbone of an organization's technology. They are the behind-the-scenes team that keeps all computers, systems, and devices working properly.

Typical Responsibilities & Daily Activities

  • Answering support requests
  • Fixing computer or software issues
  • Setting up/managing devices for employees
  • Monitoring systems to catch problems early
  • Running backups to protect data

Identify Key Technologies & Tools Used

  • Help desk ticketing systems
  • Customer relationship management software
  • Live chat software
  • Call center software
